<h2>Overview (including “The problem” and “The solution”)</h2>
<p>One of the primary challenges faced by college students today is a lack of motivation and ability to adhere to a structured schedule. Many find it challenging to stay organized and focused on their academic responsibilities, often leading to procrastination and subpar performance. We believe this lack of motivation can often stem from the difficulty of finding the right career path to meet a student’s needs and interests. Our web app seeks to simplify this search by offering comprehensive career information, including opportunities from organizations affiliated with UH, and general knowledge about career fields from both an educational and post-educational standpoint.</p>

<h2>Use case ideas</h2>
<ul>
    <li>User enters at the Landing Page and can log in to access saved career searches and edit interests</li>
    <li>When performing a search (from the Landing, Home, or Browser Page), user picks majors, specializations, and interests from category filters they’re interested in</li> 
    <li>The Browser Page presents career info in a Profile Card-based format, filtered by search query</li>
    <li>Organizations and higher users can edit their info on the Admin Page</li>
</ul>
<h2>Beyond the basics</h2>
	<p><b>Academic Path Search</b> - a second search tab that gives info on the actual courses in a student's academic career path 
        <ul>
            <li>Allow alumni and professors to share their experiences in a field</li>
            <li>Provide inspiration within a field by presenting student projects</li>
        </ul>
	<p><b>Custom Notifications</b> - Users can follow organizations to receive pings based on their participation in career fairs, upcoming opportunities, etc.</p>
